How to

Build an exhaustive list of Identify prospects.

With LinkDB , a customer can build a list of prospects by filter down an exhaustive dataset of publicly accessible LinkedIn profiles with an ideal customer profile constraint.

Grow your company with a list of prospects

Now that we have a list of prospects, a growth-stage company can build growth in four ways

Email Campaign

Cold email campaigns, how?

For each prospect found with LinkDB, use our Work Email Lookup API to fetch verfied work emails for cold email campaigns

Launch a cold email campaign

Facebook

Facebook Ads

Facebook ads at an order of magnitude cheaper and more pervasive than LinkedIn ads.

This is how:

Build a list of personal email addresses for each prospect found with LinkDB by using our Personal Email Lookup API to fetch personal (Facebook) emails of prospects

Build a custom audience on Facebook Ad Manager with the email list

Launch a FB ad campaign targeting prospects you found and similar people like such
