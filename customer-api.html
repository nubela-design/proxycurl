Customer API / use-case Story

How Proxycurl poached customers from our competitors with a price match guarantee

People Data Labs (PDL) is one of Proxycurl's close competitors. For some time there have been ex-PDL customers that switched over to Proxycurl on their own as they found Proxycurl products to be a better solution. So we thought, why not poach PDL's customers at scale?

We used the Customer API to retrieve a list of PDL's customers, enriched the customers with profile data and email addresses, and then reached out to them with a price match guarantee, offering them the same price as what they're currently paying for their PDL plans.

Continue story

How does it work?

We identify and prioritize the biggest companies using LinkDB's Company Dataset

Working down the list, we employ public and private signals aggregated via various proprietary means to identify plausible corporate customers of these companies.

We also identified individual followers who are followers on the socials of these companies.

This dataset of customers and followers, is what we call the Customer API.
