FAQ

Do you sell subsets of the data?

We do not sell subsets of the data other than by the countries.

What segments are available?

LinkDB is available for people segmented by several countries; otherwise worldwide. For companies , it is only available worldwide.

Can I resell LinkDB?

Sorry. We do not sell LinkDB to resellers.
